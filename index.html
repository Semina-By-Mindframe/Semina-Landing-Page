What Semina Does

Built around how you actually care for plants

Four core tools that cover everything — from the moment you spot a sick leaf to tracking its full recovery.

01
Plant Health Scanner
Point your camera at any plant and get an instant read on what's going on — species ID, care tips, and early warnings before problems get worse.
02
AR Plant Placement
See exactly how a plant will look and fit in your space before you bring it home. Check light levels and spacing — no more bad placement surprises.
03
Smart Sensor Kit
Bluetooth sensors sit in your soil and send live moisture, temperature, and nutrient readings straight to your phone. You'll know exactly when to water — not before, not after.
04
Plant Logbook
Every watering, repotting, and health note in one place. A full timeline for each plant so you can look back and actually learn what works.

Why It Works

Stop guessing. Start growing.

Most plants die from overwatering or bad placement. Semina fixes both.

Your plants tell you what they need

The sensors read your soil in real time and flag issues before they become visible. No more poking the soil and wondering — you'll get a notification when it's actually time to water.

  • ✓
    Live soil moisture, temp and nutrients
  • ✓
    Alerts before problems get visible
  • ✓
    Works with any plant, any pot size

See it in your space before you buy

Use the AR viewer to drop any plant into your room at actual scale. Check if it fits, if it gets enough light, and how it looks — all from your phone camera.

  • ✓
    Full 3D plant models at real scale
  • ✓
    Light level overlay per location
  • ✓
    Screenshot and share your layout

A record of every plant, not just a photo

The logbook keeps a full timeline — waterings, repottings, health notes, and photos. After a few months you'll actually know what your plant likes and doesn't like.

How We Work

The Business Behind Semina

Subscriptions, hardware, and enterprise tools — three revenue streams that build on each other.

The Foundation

Three ways we make money

Each one reinforces the others — more hardware users means more subscribers, and more data makes the product better.

App Subscriptions
Monthly and annual plans give users the full Semina experience — diagnostics, personalised care plans, and community. High margin, low churn.
Sensor Hardware
Bluetooth sensor kits sold direct and through retail. Each hardware user becomes a long-term subscriber — the kit locks them into the ecosystem.
Enterprise Licences
A white-label version for nurseries, botanical gardens, and urban farms. Annual contracts, higher margins, and a credibility signal for the consumer product.
