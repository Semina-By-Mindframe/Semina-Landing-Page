What You Can Do

Four tools that make plant care easier

Each one handles a different part of looking after your plants.

Plant Health Scanner
Point your camera at any plant and find out what it is, what it needs, and whether anything looks wrong.
AR Plant Placement
See how a plant looks in your actual room before you buy it. Check if it fits and gets enough light.
Smart Sensor Kit
Sensors sit in the soil and send readings to your phone. You get a notification when it is actually time to water.
Plant Logbook
Every watering, repotting, and health note saved in one place. Look back and see what worked for each plant.

Why people like it

No more guessing

Most plants die from too much water or the wrong spot. Semina helps you avoid both.

Know exactly when to water

The soil sensor reads moisture, temperature and nutrients throughout the day. When something is off, you get a notification. No more poking the soil and hoping for the best.

Place plants before you buy them

Open the AR view, pick a plant, and drop it anywhere in your room. You can see the real size, check the light, and take a photo to share.

How Semina makes money

Three income streams that each make the others stronger.

Monthly subscriptions
Monthly and annual plans that give users the full app. This is our main source of income.
Sensor kits
Bluetooth sensor kits sold online and in stores. People who buy the hardware tend to stay as subscribers.
Enterprise plans
A version of Semina for nurseries, botanical gardens, and commercial farms. Annual contracts with higher margins.
