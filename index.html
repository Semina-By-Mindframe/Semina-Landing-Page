Powerful Features of Semina

A comprehensive toolkit to help you become a more confident plant parent — whether you are just starting out or managing a full indoor garden.

01
Plant Health Scanner
Photograph any plant to identify its species and receive immediate care recommendations. Potential issues are flagged early, before they escalate.
02
AR Plant Placement
Visualise true-to-scale 3D plant models in your home before purchasing. Assess light availability and spatial fit directly through your camera.
03
Smart Sensor Kit
Connect Bluetooth soil sensors to monitor moisture, temperature, and nutrients in real time. Receive automated alerts when your plants require attention.
04
Plant Logbook
Maintain a complete care history for each plant — watering events, repottings, health notes, and photographs — all in one chronological timeline.
