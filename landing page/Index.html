Powerful Features of Semina

A comprehensive toolkit to help you become a more confident plant parent — whether you are just starting out or managing a full indoor garden.

Plant Health Scanner
Photograph any plant to identify its species and receive immediate care recommendations. Potential issues are flagged early, before they escalate.

AR Plant Placement
Visualise true-to-scale 3D plant models in your home before purchasing. Assess light availability and spatial fit directly through your camera.

Business Model

How Semina Creates Value

A multi-layered model built on recurring subscriptions, hardware, and enterprise partnerships — rooted in genuine outcomes for plant parents worldwide.

Our Foundation

Three Core Pillars

Semina sits at the intersection of consumer wellness, IoT hardware, and mobile software — creating a defensible and scalable business over time.

Mobile Software
A recurring subscription app with plant diagnostics, personalised care schedules, and community access. High margin, low churn, and the core of our revenue model.
Hardware Sensors
Smart sensor kits sold direct-to-consumer and through retail partners. Hardware deepens user engagement and drives long-term subscription retention.
Enterprise & Partnerships
White-label platform for nurseries, botanical gardens, and urban farms. API access for research institutions and agri-technology partners.
